About

Hey there!

Hi, welcome to my little home on the web. My name is Titus Soporan and I'm a 21-year old dude from Ontario, Canada. Originally from Romania, I immigrated to Canada at the age of six.

I was first introduced to computers around 1997 and have been hooked ever since. Whether it was playing Mortal Kombat on DOS or fiddling with the hardware on my old 100MHz Dell Optiplex Gl 5100, I always had a computer nearby.

Over time my interests shifted to programming with a focus on the web. Originally I was doing client-side coding, but gradually I dived deeper into the server-side of things and found my pot of gold there.

I am a 'hacker' at heart, I love tinkering with things and seeing how they work. When I'm not working on something I'm usually thinking or reading up on: machine learning, ways of organizing and structuring the massive data on the web and providing meaningful statistics, programming language design and implementation, biology, and entrepreneurship. I believe constantly learning is a crucial part of this field of work. I am always looking for challenges and something that will push me to give my best.

My not-so-technical interests and hobbies are:

  • jammin' on my guitar and 'trying' to make a band
  • cinema, yes, i do enjoy the odd zombie show and movie
  • gardening
  • reading anything from mystery novels, to sci-fi, to biographies

Check out some work I've done. If you like what you see don't hesitate to get in in touch.

What I do

In short, I am a web developer focused on building high-end web sites utilizing the latest technologies to acheive the desired results as quickly and painlessy as possible.

My main tool of choice is Django, a Python web framework; its motto is "for perfectionists with deadlines" which, I think, fits the bill. On the front-end side of things I use jQuery and CSS2/3 coupled with HTML4/5 to make websites engaging and functional. Also, I drink from the vim kool-aid.

Technical Expertise

  • Languages: Python, PHP, Bash, CSS, HTML, Javascript, Ruby, C
  • Frameworks: Django, jQuery, Pylons, Rails
  • Operating Systems: Linux (Gentoo, Archlinux, Debian), Windows (XP/Vista/7)
  • Server Technologies: Apache, nginx, lighttpd
  • Databases: MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQLite
  • Various Software/Protocols: Vim, Git, OpenSSH, Celery
